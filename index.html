With Have It Better, You Can Make It Better

Have It Better(HIB) is the best place where you are able to create and manage your new website easier, better, and more professional. While there are many free website builders online, this is the first offline websites builder that able to be downloaded and used anytime and anywhere.

UI/UX Director

This director will let you create your own and amazing layouts, buttons, fonts, interactions, and interfaces as easy as possible. While it can also help you manage your files, it allows you to create anything you want in minutes.

Media Manager

With this feature, you can upload and manage your media files such as images and videos. Your videos could be uploaded to the websites that you are working in or uploaded on streaming sites like YouTube or Vimeo. These media files could be set well as professional ones.

Export Projects As Professional

After working for hours, you can export your files and upload them easily to the network host with your own domain name as expert developer. Also you can share them to your account in github or to your friends to let them check out that you made it.

Capability to Work in Multiple Devices

While it's a great offline tool to create your web pages, it works in most devices. it works for most devices like Windows, Apple, Linux, iPad, iPhone, Andeoid, and Any other device that accepts web browsers. To let you work anytime and anywhere and never waste your time, we made this framework for you offline to do whatever you want to make professionally.
